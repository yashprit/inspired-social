***PLEASE BE SURE THAT YOU HAVE BOTH WPMU/WP, BUDDYPRESS AND THE DEFAUL BP THEME RUNNING PROPERLY BEFORE USING THIS THEME***


HOW-TOS:

1) Unzip buddypack.zip 
2) Upload the folder 'copernicus' into your WPMU's theme directory (it should look like this after you're done ... wp-content >> themes >> avenuek9)
3) Go into your WPMU Dashboard as the admin and click on 'Themes' under 'Side Admin' located on the left navigation bar
4) Active the Avenue K9 theme by clicking on the 'Yes' radio button and click the 'Update Themes' button below
5) Next, click on 'Themes' under 'Appearance' in the left navigation panel and click on the Avenue K9 thumbnail, which will prompt a preview of what your BP site will look like. 
6) Click on "Avenue K9" 
7) Marvel at all its glory =)  ... you may have to refresh your browser to see the change 

CHANGING THE HEADER IMAGE:
Navigate to the avenuek9 theme folder and replace the header.gif file (1250x125 pixels)
